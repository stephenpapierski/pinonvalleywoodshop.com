Giving Back

Pinon Valley Woodshop is commited to giving back to our community

Student Mission Auction

Each year, Woodmen Valley Chapel holds an auction to raise money for their students going on short term missions trips. We make it a habbit to contribute an item each year.

2024 - Cornhole Boards

These custom made cornhole boards are one of a kind and ready for your next backyard barbeque or tailgate. They include all weather cornhole bags and custom "Love Well" Woodmen themed artwork on the decks. The boards are made from premium baltic birch plywood, which means you get a reliable playing surface with no bounce. Hand crafted in Colorado Springs by Pinon Valley Woodshop. This piece has yet to be sold, so it could be yours! Find out more about the in-person auction being held on March 3rd, 2024 here!

2023 - Coffee Table

This hand made coffee table features a solid red oak top finished in a repairable, environmentally friendly, hard-wax oil. The oil protects the wood, while allowing the natural beauty of the wood to shine through with a matte finish. The top is complimented by black powder coated steel legs with a unique "Double-Z" shape. This piece raised $250 for the students!
